Frequently Asked Questions:

Why is a pool skimmer important?

A pool skimmer is essential for maintaining clean and clear pool water by removing surface debris before it sinks to the bottom. It helps prevent clogging of the filtration system, reduces the need for manual cleaning, and ensures a more enjoyable swimming experience.

Can I use the pool skimmer to fish for my baby?

While our pool skimmers are incredibly efficient, they're not equipped with a "sunglasses retrieval mode." We recommend using your good old fishing skills or just investing in a new pair!

Will the pool skimmer double as a makeshift jacuzzi for my pet goldfish?

While your goldfish might appreciate the gesture, our pool skimmers are designed for larger bodies of water. We suggest sticking to a traditional fishbowl or investing in a proper fish spa for your aquatic friends!

How does the filter fit into the main part?

Simply hold the handle and clip it into the bottom center of the main body of your PoolBoy. It will hold there, and when you want to remove it, just use the handle to pull it back out!
