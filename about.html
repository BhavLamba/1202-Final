Who We Are

Here at SkimSmart Technologies, we work hard to design and develop cutting edge pool products perfect for residential and commercial aquatic applications. Formed in 2024 by aspiring Mechanical Engineering Majors at the prestigious University of North Carolina at Charlotte and based in Charlotte, North Carolina, SkimSmart is at the forefront of pool product development and working hard to service pools across the nation with top-rate appliances.
